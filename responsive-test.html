Testing Checklist:

  • Mobile: All content visible without horizontal scroll
  • Mobile: Text sizes readable and not too large
  • Mobile: Padding not excessive, buttons clickable
  • Mobile: Images visible on right side (stacked below on very small screens)
  • Tablet: Layout transitions smoothly at md breakpoint
  • Desktop: Two-column layout (left bank info, right image slider)
  • All sizes: No overlapping elements
  • All sizes: No cut-off text or containers
